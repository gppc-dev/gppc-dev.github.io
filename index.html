Grid-Based Path Planning Competition

The Competition

That Grid-based Path Pinding is a widely studied topic with many practical applications. It simplicity also makes it an excellent testbed for algorithmic evaluation. Many papers appear on this topic every year. The purpose the GPPC is to provide a common experimental setup that allows researchers and practitioners to evaluate progress in the area.

The 2021 GPPC is the fourth edition of the competition, with previous competitions in 2012, 2013 and 2014.
